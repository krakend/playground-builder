## About this demo
Grafana Monitoring shows the usage of KrakenD and reveals low-level metrics.

Access the demo: [http://localhost:4000](http://localhost:4000)

Use `krakend` as temporary user and password. And then go to `Dashboards` > `Manage` to see the KrakenD dashboard.

![Grafana screenshot](https://www.krakend.io/images/documentation/screenshots/grafana-prometheus-otel.png)
